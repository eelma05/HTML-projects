Chicken Noodle Stir-Fry.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chickencut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 450g of Noodles.
  • 3tbsp of Soy Sauce.
  • 2 tbsp of Sugar.
  • 2 tbsp of Sesame Oil.
  • 4 slices of Ginger (sliced)
  • 6 cloves of Garlic (sliced)
  • 1 Red Chili (chopped)

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar till combined.
  2. Cook noodle as per instruction, drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, drizzle in oil and turn the heat up to medium-high. Once the pan is hot, add in the chicken and saute together for 3-4 minutes.
  4. After 3-4 minutes, add in sliced ginger, garlic, and chopped red chili. Saute together for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add in the noodles and pour in the sauce.
  6. Serve while still hot.
